GUIDE

  • The maze grid can be set up with a minimum size of 4x4 and a maximum size of 16x16.
  • Ensure the maze has exactly one entry and one exit.
  • The entry and exit cannot be positioned at the corners of the maze. They must be placed along the edges but not in any corner cells.
  • Drag around the boxes on the grid to form the walls of the maze.
  • Click on a box to toggle between a wall and a free space/path.
  • You can click on the "Load Maze" button to save the current maze configuration.
  • A maximum of 5 mazes can be stored. If you attempt to save more, older mazes will be overwritten.
  • After creating the maze, click on the "Solve" button to solve the maze
  • If you want to start over or remove the solved path, click the "Reset" button
